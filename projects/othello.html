Rules

  • Black goes first
  • On your turn, place one disk on the gameboard
  • Available places you may place your disk will highlight in yellow when hovered over
  • Any of the opponents disks that are between the one you place and the next of your color will flip to your color
  • After one player places a tile, it is the next player's turn
  • Once all tiles have disks on them, the game is over
  • Whichever player has the most tiles on the board at the end of the game wins
